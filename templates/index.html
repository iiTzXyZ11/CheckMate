TIPS FOR ACCURACY

Provide a Clear Assignment Prompt

Describe the main topic, focus areas, and any themes to address. This allows students to approach the topic in different ways while staying relevant.

List Key Terms, Concepts, and Exceptions

Include essential vocabulary and concepts without restricting language, allowing students to use varied expressions while remaining accurate to the topic.

Clarify Depth of Analysis Expected

Specify if students should provide a high-level overview or a deeper analysis. This encourages students to explore different perspectives and levels of detail without straying off-topic.
